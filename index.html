ABOUT US

ADENIYI HALL aka Palace of Elites, a vibrant and comfortable accommodation facility located within the Federal University of Technology Akure campus.

Adeniyi is a home to all whether you're a new student or a returning resident, we are here to provide you with a safe and enjoyable living experience.

Adeniyi is situated at the heart of the university campus, after Aluta Market Road. It's located in-front of Jadesola hostel and beside Futascoop and Jibowu Annex 3 hostels. You have easy access to buying food and beverages, 24-hour light, and running water.
